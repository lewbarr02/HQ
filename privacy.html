Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

HQ is a personal productivity application built and used solely by Lewis Barr. This policy describes how data is handled.

Data Collected

  • All app data (habits, routines, goals, logs) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
  • Push notification subscriptions are stored in a private Supabase database accessible only to the app owner.
  • SMS notifications are sent via Twilio to a single pre-configured phone number owned by the app owner.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is not sold. The only third-party services used are Supabase (database/functions) and Twilio (SMS delivery), solely for app functionality.

SMS Messaging

SMS messages are sent only to the app owner's personal mobile number. Message frequency varies but is typically 4–10 messages per day. Standard message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.
